About MGTECHNZ

Local technology experts helping NZ businesses succeed since 20XX

We Deliver Excellence In Technology

Based in the Waikato region, MGTECHNZ is a proudly New Zealand owned and operated technology company. We combine years of technical experience with friendly, honest, and clear service — no jargon, no confusion, just results.

We started with one goal: to make high-quality, reliable technology accessible and affordable for small and medium-sized businesses across New Zealand. Whether you need a simple website, ongoing IT support, or complex custom systems, we build solutions that fit *your* business, not the other way around.

Our team works remotely and on-site, serving clients from Northland to Southland. We understand the unique needs of NZ businesses — and we’re here to help you grow, work faster, and stay secure.
